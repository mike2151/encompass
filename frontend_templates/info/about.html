About Us

Encompass Interviews is an online platform leading the charge to change the way technical programming interview questions are done. We believe that the current way that programming interviews are done, primarily through algorithm based interviews, do not stress skills that are important in an actual programming job. This leads to a poor signal for the company and high stress for the applicant. We attempt to change this by offering different types of questions on our platform that stress skills such as reading existing code, using APIs, and solving implementation challenges.
